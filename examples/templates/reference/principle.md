# Document title

## Principle

The principle itself, stated in one or two memorable sentences.

## Rationale

Why this principle exists — the experience, values, or constraints
behind it.

## Implications

What following this principle looks like in practice: the decisions it
pushes toward and the ones it pushes away from.

## Tensions and trade-offs

What this principle costs, and the other principles or pressures it can
conflict with. Say how conflicts should be weighed.

## Examples

Real decisions where this principle applied, and what it led to.

## Related

Standards that enforce aspects of this principle, and neighbouring
principles.
