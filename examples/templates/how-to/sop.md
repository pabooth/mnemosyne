# Document title

## Purpose

Why this procedure exists and what obligation (regulatory, contractual,
internal policy) it satisfies.

## Scope

The activities, systems, and people this procedure applies to, and any
explicit exclusions.

## Roles and responsibilities

Who performs the procedure, who approves or witnesses it where required,
and who owns it.

## Procedure

1. Numbered steps, in order. Each step states who performs it and what
   evidence, if any, it produces.
2. Mark any step where deviation requires prior approval.

## Records

The records this procedure produces, where they are stored, and how long
they are retained.

## Compliance

How adherence is monitored or audited, and the consequences of
non-compliance.

## Exceptions

The process for requesting a deviation, who approves it, and how the
deviation is documented.

## Review

Review cycle, next review date, and the owner responsible.
