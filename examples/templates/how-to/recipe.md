# Document title

## Goal

The single outcome this recipe achieves, in one or two sentences.

## Prerequisites

What the reader must have or know before starting. Assume competence;
link to background rather than explaining it.

## Steps

1. Each step is one action with an observable result.
2. Keep steps in strict order; call out any step that is destructive
   or hard to reverse.

## Verification

How to confirm the goal was achieved.

## Troubleshooting

The failures most likely to occur, and what to do about each.

## Related

Neighbouring recipes, the concepts behind this task, and relevant reference
material.
