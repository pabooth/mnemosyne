# Document title

## Status

Proposed, accepted, or superseded (with a link to the successor), and the
date of the decision.

## Context

The situation that forces a decision: the problem, the constraints, and
why deciding now matters.

## Options considered

Each option with its genuine pros and cons — including the option chosen.
An ADR with only one option recorded is a press release, not a decision
record.

## Decision

The choice made, stated plainly.

## Justification

Why this option won: the reasoning that connects the context to the
choice.

## Consequences

What follows from the decision — the good, the costs accepted, and any
follow-up work it creates.

## Related

The decisions this one builds on, amends, or supersedes.
