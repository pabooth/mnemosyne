# Document title

## Summary

The proposal in a few sentences, readable without the rest of the
document.

## Motivation

The problem or opportunity: what is wrong or missing today, who it
affects, and the cost of doing nothing.

## Proposal

The change being argued for, in enough detail that a reader could
evaluate it — and disagree with it — concretely.

## Alternatives

The other ways to address the motivation, each with a fair account of
its merits and why the proposal is preferred.

## Consequences

What adopting this changes — benefits, costs, risks, and who bears each.
Include the migration or adoption path if the change is not free.

## Unresolved questions

The parts of the proposal that comment is specifically invited on.

## Decision

Left empty while open. On resolution: the outcome, the date, and a link
to the ADR that records the decision if one was produced.
