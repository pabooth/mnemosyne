# Document title

## Overview

The system in one paragraph: what it is for and its overall shape.

## Context and constraints

The environment the design lives in — the systems around it, the
requirements it serves, and the fixed points it had to respect.

## Architecture

The components, their responsibilities, and the boundaries between them.
A diagram is usually worth including here.

## Data flow

How information moves through the system: the main paths, their triggers,
and where state lives.

## Key decisions

The load-bearing choices in this design, each linking to its ADR where
one exists.

## Alternatives considered

The shapes this system deliberately is not, and why.

## Risks and limitations

Where the design is fragile, what it does not handle, and what would
force it to change.

## Open questions

What remains unresolved in the design, and who owns each question.
