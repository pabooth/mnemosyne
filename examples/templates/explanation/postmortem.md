# Document title

## Summary

The incident in a few sentences: what broke, for how long, and the impact.

## Impact

Who and what was affected, for how long, and how severely. Quantify where
possible.

## Timeline

The sequence of events, timestamped, from first cause through detection
and response to resolution. Facts only — analysis comes later.

## Root causes

Why this happened. Look past the trigger to the conditions that allowed
it; there is usually more than one cause. Blameless: name causes in
systems and processes, not people.

## What went well

The parts of the response worth keeping and repeating.

## What went badly

Where detection, response, or communication fell short.

## Actions

| | | | |

Each action should remove a cause or shorten a future response — not just
"be more careful".

## Related

The runbooks exercised (or found missing), prior related incidents, and
the changelog entries for the fixes.
