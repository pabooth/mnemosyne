# Document title

## Where we are

The honest current state: what works, what does not, and the pressures
driving change.

## Where we are going

The target state, and the horizon over which it is meant to be reached.

## Approach

How we get from here to there — the bets being made and the reasoning
behind them.

## Priorities

The ordered tracks of work this strategy commits to, and what is
deliberately deferred.

## Measures

How progress will be judged: the signals that say the strategy is working,
and the ones that say it is not.

## Risks

What could invalidate this strategy, and what is being watched for.

## Review

When and by whom this strategy is revisited.
